About Milago Deluge


Hi, I’m Milagro Deluge. I am sixteen, and I am from Dominica.


I love spending time with my family—it’s always nice to share laughs and create memories together.


In my free time, I enjoy reading, playing the guitar, and sewing. Reading introduces me to new ideas and stories, while sewing gives me the chance to be creative and craft one-of-a-kind items. These activities let me express my creativity in different ways. I also love cooking, especially trying out new recipes or making something special for loved ones.


I love animals and enjoy playing with my pets whenever I get the chance. Going on walks is another favorite activity—it’s relaxing and a great way to enjoy nature. I also love decorating spaces to make them feel cozy and beautiful. like to do my hair and experiment with new styles, which is a fun way for me to be creative. I also love learning new things, whether it’s trying a new hobby or discovering something interesting about the world.
