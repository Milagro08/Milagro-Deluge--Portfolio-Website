Milagro Deluge: Education


My education has been a valuable experience that has helped me develop and get ready for what lies ahead.


It started at the Convent Preparatory School, where I learned the basics like reading, writing, and math. My time there built a strong foundation and made me excited to learn more.


For secondary school, I attended the Convent High School, where I worked hard to excel in my studies. I enjoyed the many activities offered and learned how to manage my time and work well with others. When I sat the CSEC examinations, I was proud to earn 7 ones and 2 twos, showing my dedication and effort.


Now, I am a student at the Dominica State College, majoring in Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science. These subjects match my interests and help me gain knowledge and skills for the future. College has been a great experience, giving me the chance to work on projects, improve my lab skills, and prepare for my career.
